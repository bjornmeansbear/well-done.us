Well Done is a retail gallery space that showcases collections of lifestyle objects based on changing themes. The showroom presents these objects as a collection in a physical environment similar an art display. This approach mirrors the type of curation that is often seen online through means of Tumblr, Twitter, personal blogs and websites.

The premier and current exhibit, “For The Ladies,” contains objects and apparel for women in their twenties. The items on display include basics, personal accessories, publications and home decor making the goal to provide a cohesive series of objects tailored to a specific lifestyle.

Through ongoing and changing exhibitions, Well Done’s goal is to create a meaningful dialogue between the customer and the necessities that are on display, but also to promote a more comprehensive approach to styling.

— Well Done.

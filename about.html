About

Well Done is a pop up store for women in Station North, that will bring people tangible objects– things that they did not know they would be interested in.

By creating this platform we have called out the type of curation that is often seen online through means of tumblr, twitter, personal blogs and websites, in which the audience receives this information in physical form. The information that we have curated becomes inclusive and does not get regurgitated over and over again as user generated content does on the internet.

Through a series of changing curated exhibitions, Well Done’s goal is to create a meaningful dialogue between the customer and objects.
